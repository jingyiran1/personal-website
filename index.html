The Journey

From electrical engineering labs in China to product analytics in New York — a winding, wonderful pivot.

2013 – 2017 · China
B.S. Electrical Engineering
China
Spent four years deep in circuits and signal processing — learning how to think rigorously about systems, which turned out to be the perfect foundation for working with data.
2017 – 2019 · Pittsburgh, PA
M.S. Business Analytics, Carnegie Mellon
Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
Traded resistors for regressions. CMU's Tepper School opened up a new world — statistics, machine learning, and how data actually drives business decisions.
2019 – 2022 · New York, NY
Data Scientist, Consulting
New York, New York
Worked across industries — from financial services to healthcare — helping clients turn messy data into actionable strategy. Learned that context is everything in analytics.
2022 – Present · New York, NY
Product Data Scientist, Tech
New York, New York
Now building measurement frameworks, running experiments, and shaping product decisions at scale — the perfect blend of technical depth and human impact.
